Itinerary and (Optional) Programme 2003

 

 

Day 1 - Monday 14 July 
We fly from HEATHROW to HELSINKI and then an internal flight to JOENSUU & our coach to take us to the modern, NEW VALAMO MONASTERY for dinner bed & breakfast.

 

Day 2 - Tuesday 
After Mass we visit the monastery & it’s visitor facility including an excellent shop. Transfer to KUOPIO, followed by a short presentation of Lintula, next the Kupio Orthodox episcopate & museum. Finally IISALMI EVAKKOKESKUS & dinner with Fr Elias in the Pilgrims centre. Back to Valamo for the night.
