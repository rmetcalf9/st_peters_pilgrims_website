MEDJUGORJE

Eight days (7 nights)

Half Board - By schedule flights of Air Croatia

Pilgrimage Reference  MJ8

Pray and do Penance for Peace

 

Our departure dates 2005:-

We regret only available for Parish Groups (20 Pilgrims +) It is possible to arrange this Pilgrimage at any date and duration to suit your Group.

 

For individuals - Dates for groups where you may join will be displayed

Itinerary and (Optional) Programme 2005

 

Day 1
 3.00 pm Check-in at the Gatwick Airport.
5.00 pm Air Croatia flight OU495 takes off. 
8.15 pm Land at Split Airport our coach will be waiting to take us to our accommodation in Medjugorje. 

NOTE:-  SEE  BELOW  FOR  SEPTEMBER  PILGRIMAGES  FLIGHTS>

Day 2
 9.00 am Breakfast 
10.00 am We will join the English Mass in the church of St. James. After Mass there will be a group photo and a guided visit to the Sanctuaries around the Church.
1.00 pm You are free to purchase your lunch.
The rest of the day free for your own personal religious devotions or we will make the Way of the Cross on the flat situated by the sculpture of the Risen Christ. 
8.00 pm Dinner
9.00 pm After dinner group the Rosary. To night we will take time to pray together as a group

Day 3
     am Early morning we depart on foot for Mount Krizevac. We follow the way of the Cross on Mount Krizevac. 
9.00 am Breakfast
10.00 am English Mass in St. James’ Church.
Afternoon free
6.00 pm Rosary, 
7.00 pm Croatian Mass
8.00 pm Dinner
Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament.

Day 4
 9.00am Breakfast.
10.00am English Mass in St. James’ Church.Rest of the morning free for private prayer, confessions etc.
2.00 pm Our coach departs for our visit to Fra Jozo Zovko (parish priest of Medjugorje during the time of the apparitions) in Siroki Brijeg.Fr. Jozo will address the group.
6.00 pm Rosary
7.00 pm Croatian Mass.
8.00 pm Dinner
After dinner group Rosary Blessing of Religious Objects and Healing Service & Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament.

Day 5
 7.00 am Breakfast.
7.30 am Croatian Mass in the Church of St. James.
10.00 am We will attend the English Mass in St. James Church.
4.00 pm We walk to Mount Podbrdo (Hill of the Apparitions) to join the local Priest & people in the Rosary and climbing to the place where our Lady first appeared.
8.00 pm Dinner
Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament.

Day 6
 9.00 am Breakfast
10.00 am We will attend the English Mass in St. James Church.
The rest of the day free for your own personal prayer.Or you may take an optional excursion, our local guide will give you the details.
8.00 pm Dinner
Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament.

Day 7
 9.00 am Breakfast 
10.00 am We will join the English Mass in the church of St. James. The rest of the day free. Should the be a talk by one of the local priest we will attend this. Our local guide will keep us informed. Visit the visionaries.

Day 8
 7.00 am Breakfast 
7.30 am Croatian Mass in the Church of St. James.
9.00 am Our coach departs Medjugorje.
12.45 pm We check in Split Airport.
2.45 pm Air Croatia flight OU494 takes off
4.15 pm We land at Gatwick Airport.

Flight times September details:

TUESDAY 28 SEPTEMBER
 2.05 pm Check-in at the Gatwick South Airport 
4.05 pm Air Croatia flight OU499 takes off. 
7.35 pm Land at DUBROVNIK Airport our coach will be waiting to take us to our accommodation in Medjugorje. 

TUESDAY 5 OCTOBER.
 8.30 am Our coach departs Medjugorje.
11.40 am We check in DUBROVNIK Airport.
1.40 pm Air Croatia flight OU498 takes off
3.20 pm We land at Gatwick Airport.

 


The Blue Cross		 Prices:   

May £419   June & September £408

What’s included.  Return flights. All Government & airport taxes. Transfers & coach journeys shown in the itinerary. Half board accommodation based on a twin room. A local English speaking guide.

What’s not included.  Insurance.   Single-room supplement £7 per night.

Our accommodation:- With local families. We will be provided with continental breakfast and a two course evening meal.

Coming from outside the UK?  If you would like to fly direct to Split and meet us at the airport there is a discount of £150.00 on these prices.
