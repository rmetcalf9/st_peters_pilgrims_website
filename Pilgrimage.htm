We are a family organistaion based in London, England. We were founded twenty five years ago in 1979. by Bob & Mary Metcalf to take Pilgrims to Lourdes. Bob had been a Pilgrim to Lourdes since 1966, was a titular member of the Hospitalitie of Our Lady of Lourdes, & Chief Brancardier of the Annual Cheshire Homes Pilgrimage for the sick, & felt there was a lack of a good service for ordinary Pilgrims.)  We soon started to arrange regular Pilgrimages to all the great centers shown on the left in friendly groups of between 30 & 45 Pilgrims. Each led by a Catholic Priest and accompanied by Bob or Mary or one of our experienced & dedicated Representatives. We welcome Parish groups, families and individual Pilgrims from all over the world to join us either in London or at our Pilgrimage destination. Select from the list to find out more about us, our destinations,  our Pilgrimages and all the practical information & advice you will need & plenty of links to other information sites and the shrines themselves.

 We are a specialist Pilgrimage organisation not a general travel agent.

15/3/05 - 

Sorry we are late but we have now started to update our web site which will take a few days.

 

Important - having operated all aspects of our Pilgrimages for 25 years and taken care of over 110,000 Pilgrimage journeys we are continuing to scale down. For 2005 we will be operating our Pilgrimages in conjunction with Pax Travel Ltd. who will handle bookings, travel and accommodation. Pax Travel are licensed by the British Civil Aviation Authority, ATOL license No. 3011 so you are fully protected under their bond.

 

This will be reflected on all our web pages as soon as they are updated.

 

We will be operating very much the same Pilgrimage programs in 2005 as we have done in previous years.
