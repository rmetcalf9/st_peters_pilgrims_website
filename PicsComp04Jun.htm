SANTIAGO DE COMPOSTELA - 7th June, 2004

1. We visit the house of St. Ignatious in Loyola

2 We celerbrate one of our Masses in The village of Castrojeriz

3 Fr. meets his match in Ponferrada

4 We enjoy a good meal together.

5 And keep company with Mr. Gaudi in Leon

6 A good cup of tea in Spain. (Or was it coffee?)

7 We collect our stones for the high altitude, Cruz de Ferro

8 Yes, all of us!

9 Mary and Helen with Pablo our driver

10 The pile of stones at the Cruz get higher

11 And we pray for all the Pilgrims who have put them there

12 A great place for photos.

13 At last the Hill of Joy and our first view of Santiago

14 But those Pilgrims are big!

15 Our Santiago guide shows us the Cathedral and town

16 While we listen on

17 Then enjoy a break

18 Intense discussion

19 Continued in our hotel!

20 Here comes the Pyella 

21 The Pilgrims at the Cathedral door.
