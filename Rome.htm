OUR HOTELS IN ITALY:-  

 

DELLA CONCILIAZIONE (3 star). ROME.

This is a standard city center hotel, all rooms with toilet & shower/bath, direct dial telephone, some rooms with TV. Hairdryer is available from Reception. It provides Bed & Breakfast. There are many bars & restaurants in the same street. Situated 200 meters from St. Peter's Square. A safe deposit box is available from reception for a small charge. Lift to all floors

NOVA DOMUS (4 star). ROME

All rooms are fully air-conditioned with private toilet, shower/bath, mini bar, direct dial telephone, satellite TV, hair dryer, safe deposit box & sound proofing. There is a restaurant, Bar & roof garden. Lift to all floors

DOMUS PACIS (3 star). ASSISI.

A Franciscan hotel situated near the Basilica of Santa Maria degli Angels . All rooms with private toilet, shower/bath, direct dial telephone. Bar & Lounge. Lift to all floors.

SAN PIETRO  (3 star). ASSISI

Situated by the San Pietro entrance to the upper town of Assisi near the Basilica of St. Francis. All rooms with private toilet, shower/bath, hairdryer, direct dial telephone & TV, safe deposit box, mini bar. Bar & restaurant. Lift to all floors (some rooms are reach by steps). Family run hotel.

FINI  (3 star)  SAN GIOVANNI ROTONDO

This is modern architectural style. All rooms with private toilet, shower/bath, direct dial telephone & TV. Bar & restaurant. Family run hotel.
